A star is born

The story begin in 1986, Usain bolt grows up with is parent. At first, he's more a cricket and football guy until he shows his sprinting potential running in the national primary-school's meeting for his parish.

Hopeful debut

At the age of 14 he manages to win a medal on the 200m and also set his personnal best on the 400m. He win a couple more of medals and, at 15 years and 334 days he becomes the youngest youth world champ on the 200m. US universities offer him scholarships but he refuses and prefers study at the university of technology in Kingston.

Confirmation

He joins the training group hold by Glenn Mills. Observers start to call him "Lightning bolt". Despite some injuries he achieves to set a new national jama√Øcan record on the 200m and starts to be problematic for other sprinters.

First world record break

He first beats the world record by 2 hundredth over his compatriot Asafa Powell then, at the Olympic Games in Pekin, he wins three gold medals setting some new records on 100m, 200m and 4x100m.

Zenith

He was already the first man to break the 9.70s bar on 100m. But that wasn't enough. At the world championship in Berlin he sets a new record on the 100m (9s58) and the 200m (19s19), he also wins 4x100 with his compatriotes.

Truncate season

Not much on this season. Even if he tries to do some races, he gets injured multiple times and gets beaten by Tyson Gay breaking his winning streak.

2/3

He's back on track in the world championship holded in Daegu. He wins a gold on the 4x100m and 200m but gets disqualified on the 100m for a false start.

London

Back for his second Olympic games for 3 new medals on 100m, 200m and 4x100m. He's also elected athlete of the year for the 4th time.

Worlds in Moscow

Despite a difficult season beginning he succeeds to wins another triple at the world championship. Moreover, he's again named athlete of year for the fifth time.

Silent season

Injured on this season, not much happened, after only 4 races he decides to put an end on his season to focus on the next one.

Back to the country of rising sun

He achieves what he did on his first trip back in 2008 and wins gold medals on 100m, 200m and 4x100 (as usual).

The third triple

This year marks his third Olympic games where he again, wins another triple making him the biggest sprinter of all time achieving his Olympic career with a 100% victory rate.

The last season

After doping control announcement the jamaican team loses her medal on 4x100m won in 2008. That's his last season where he only wishes run on the 100m and 4x100m. He finishes at the 3rd place and gets injured on the 4x100m putting an end point to his sprinting career.
